The worthwhile way to waitlist

Whether you're a restaurant, beauty salon or event agency, Waitwhile™ gives you a smart and simple waitlist with customizable SMS alerts. It's about time to make your customers happier and your business run smoother.

Waitlist from anywhere

Manage your waitlist from your desktop, tablet or phone, even without WiFi. It's as simple as a tap.

Keep guests informed

Guests can run errands while waiting and check the wait on their phone, no install needed.

Notify with SMS

Just text or email guests once you're ready. If they reply, it shows up in your waitlist.

Check-in station

Easily set up a check-in station for customers to add themselves - online or in your store.

Customize everything

Make your waitlist reflect your business and update your SMS texts with guest personalization.

Wait time & analytics

Get accurate wait times, spot waitlist trends and learn about your customers to improve your business.
